Generating Private Keys and SSL Certs

$ openssl req  -new  -newkey rsa:2048  -nodes  -keyout localhost.key  -out localhost.csr

$ openssl  x509  -req  -days 365  -in localhost.csr  -signkey localhost.key  -out localhost.crt

Store cert in trusted root:

Windows:

>certutil -addstore -f "ROOT" new-root-certificate.crt

Linux:
	
	1. copy CA to dir /usr/local/share/ca-certificates/
	2. $ sudo cp foo.crt /usr/local/share/ca-certificates/foo.crt
	3. $ sudo update-ca-certificates

Configuring HTTPS server:

	log.Fatal( http.ListenAndServeTLS(":9000", "localhost.crt", "localhost.key", nil))
